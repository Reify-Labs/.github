# Reify Labs

We build apps that use reality as a surface for technology — augmenting the physical world rather than replacing it.

Our products are social by design, grounded in real places, and built to bring people together IRL.

## What We Build

- **Social & discovery apps** — helping people find what's happening around them and show up for it
- **Location-based multiplayer games** — shared experiences anchored to the real world
- **Reality-augmented experiences** — technology layered on top of everyday life

## Mission

Technology should make the real world richer, not a place you escape from.
